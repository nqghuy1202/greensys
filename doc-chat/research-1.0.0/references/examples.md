## Interview Discussion Guide Template

### Discussion Guide: [Topic]

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes
**Participant Type:** [Description]

#### Introduction (5 min)

```
Hi [Name], thank you for taking the time to speak with me today.
My name is [Researcher] and I'm researching [topic].

Before we start:
- This will take about [X] minutes
- There are no right or wrong answers - we want your honest opinions
- I'll be recording this session for note-taking purposes
- Your responses are confidential and will be anonymized
- You can skip any question or stop at any time

Do you have any questions before we begin?
Do I have your consent to record?
```

#### Warm-up (5 min)

- Tell me a little about yourself and your role
- How long have you been [relevant context]?

#### Topic 1: [Name] (10-15 min)

**Goal:** Understand [specific aspect]

1. Walk me through the last time you [relevant activity]
   - What were you trying to accomplish?
   - What tools/methods did you use?
   - What worked well? What was frustrating?

2. Tell me about a time when [scenario]
   - What happened?
   - How did you feel?
   - What did you do next?

#### Topic 2: [Name] (10-15 min)

**Goal:** Explore [specific aspect]

1. How do you currently [activity]?
   - Probe: What's the hardest part about that?

2. If you could change one thing about [topic], what would it be?
   - Probe: Why is that important to you?

#### Topic 3: [Name] (10-15 min)

**Goal:** Validate [specific aspect]

1. [Specific question about concept/feature]
2. What would you expect to happen when [scenario]?

#### Wrap-up (5 min)

- Is there anything else about [topic] that I should have asked?
- Any questions for me?

```
Thank you so much for your time today. Your insights are really valuable.
We'll send your [incentive] within [timeframe].
```

## Affinity Mapping Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Preparation

**Materials needed:**
- Sticky notes (one color per participant or session)
- Wall space or digital board (Miro, FigJam)
- Markers
- 2-4 hours of team time

**Data to include:**
- Key quotes from transcripts
- Observations
- Participant statements
- Behavioral notes

#### 2. Note Creation

Write one observation per sticky note:

**Good note format:**
```
P3: "I always check my email first thing because
I'm worried I'll miss something important"
```

**Bad note format:**
```
Users check email (too vague, no source)
```

#### 3. Clustering Process

1. **Silent sorting** (20 min)
   - Team members move notes into groups
   - No talking - let patterns emerge
   - It's okay to move others' notes

2. **Discussion** (20 min)
   - Review each cluster
   - Discuss disagreements
   - Merge or split clusters

3. **Naming** (10 min)
   - Create theme statement for each cluster
   - Use actionable language
   - Keep names specific

#### 4. Theme Statement Format

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| "Email" | "Users feel anxiety about missing important communications" |
| "Problems" | "Current workflow requires too many manual steps" |
| "Good stuff" | "Users value real-time collaboration features" |

#### 5. Documentation

Create summary with:
- Theme name
- Number of notes/sources
- Key quotes
- Implications

   - 55+

   - [Relevant option 1]
   - [Relevant option 2]
   - Other: ___
   - [Disqualifying option] [DISQUALIFY]

#### Usage Questions

3. How often do you [relevant activity]?
   - Daily [TARGET]
   - Weekly [TARGET]
   - Monthly
   - Rarely [DISQUALIFY]
   - Never [DISQUALIFY]

4. Which of these products/tools do you currently use? (Select all)
   - [Product A] [REQUIRED]
   - [Product B]
   - [Competitor C]
   - None of these [DISQUALIFY]

#### Experience Level

5. How would you rate your experience with [domain]?
   - Expert (5+ years)
   - Advanced (2-5 years)
   - Intermediate (1-2 years) [TARGET]
   - Beginner (<1 year) [TARGET]
   - No experience [DISQUALIFY]

6. Do you or does anyone in your household work for any of the following?
   - [Company name]
   - Market research firm
   - Advertising agency
   - None of the above [REQUIRED]

7. Are you available for a [X]-minute [video call / in-person session] during [dates]?
   - Yes
   - No [DISQUALIFY]
